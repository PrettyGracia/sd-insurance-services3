Cyber Insurance

SD Insurance's Cyber Insurance is a crucial coverage for businesses of all sizes in today's digital age. This insurance protects your business against the financial losses and liabilities resulting from cyber attacks, data breaches, and other cyber threats. Whether you operate a small business or a large enterprise, our Cyber Insurance provides the peace of mind you need to focus on growing your business.

Key Features of Cyber Insurance

  • Data Breach Coverage: Protection against the costs associated with data breaches, including notification expenses, credit monitoring, and legal fees.
  • Cyber Extortion: Coverage for ransom payments and related expenses in the event of a cyber extortion incident.
  • Business Interruption: Protection against loss of income and additional expenses incurred due to disruptions in your business operations caused by a cyber attack.
  • Network Security Liability: Coverage for legal liability arising from security failures that result in data breaches or transmission of malicious software.
  • Privacy Liability: Protection against claims arising from the unauthorized access or disclosure of personal information.
  • Incident Response: Access to a team of cyber security experts to help you respond to and recover from a cyber incident.
